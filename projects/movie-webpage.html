My Moive Webpage

Context

My movie webpage, The Exterminator vs. Le Rat, is an action moive about a cat and a rat against each other. The prompt was to create a fictional moive webpage where me and my partner use our knowledge of basic HTML and CSS.

Content

I made a webpage about a rat and a cat but me and my partner first created a plan to slipt up the work between us. We created a movie called The Exterminator vs. Le Rat. The exterminator is a cat who fought for the title of royalty while Le Rat is a rat who wanted to fight for revenge. We created this webpage by using HTML like heading, paragraphs, divs, and more. Along with CSS like background and text color, borders, margins, padding, and fonts.

Challenges

The project wasn't as challenging as I thought and I didnt really have many challenges especially with the help of my partner and previous lessons. Although we did struggled on thinking of an idea for the movie. We knew it was going to be an action movie but we had to decide if it was going to be based on a superhero/ villian or based on a spy. Along with that we also didnt know which colors to use for the background and characters.
