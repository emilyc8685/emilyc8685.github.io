SEP-10 Freedom Project

Context

My SEP-10 freedom project is about education. For this project I had to choose a topic (I chose education) and I had to create a website about future possible technolgy related to my topic. This was a year-long project about making a website that informs the viewer of the current and future innovations in the topic of my choosing.

Content

The product I created was the Smart Desk. The Smart Desk is a desk with a built in screen and app. I thought of future technology in education by thinking of ways students can be more engaged in learning. Many students tend to get distracted easily while studying and teachers struggle to keep students engaged in class.

Here are some of the features of my Smart Desk

  • Students can learn with videos, online books/textbooks , quizzes/tests, and class work on the app while teachers can assign classwork, quizzes, and tests
  • Teachers and parents receive progress reports to see how their students are doing
  • During class and at home teachers can give feedback/comments to better improve learning for students
